# Welcome to Yantra Launcher Wiki! 🚀

Hello there, CLI enthusiast! We're thrilled to have you here.

Yantra Launcher isn't just another Android launcher; it's a bridge between the classic efficiency of the command line and the modern versatility of your smartphone. Whether you're a seasoned terminal veteran or a curious newcomer looking to boost your productivity, you've come to the right place.

## What is Yantra Launcher?

Yantra is a minimal, yet powerful, CLI-based launcher for Android. It's designed for those who find peace in the terminal and efficiency in a well-typed command. It strips away the clutter of icons and widgets, replacing them with a sleek, responsive interface where your keyboard is the primary tool.

## Why use Yantra?

- **Blazing Fast:** Launch apps, call contacts, or search the web in seconds—no more digging through app drawers.
- **Productivity First:** Built-in tools for notes, todos, alarms, and calculators keep you in the flow.
- **Deeply Customizable:** From themes and fonts to custom aliases and scripts, make the terminal truly yours.
- **Power User Ready:** Execute Lua scripts, integrate with Termux, and automate your daily tasks with `init` scripts.
